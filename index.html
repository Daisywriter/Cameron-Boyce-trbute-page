Cameron Boyce

~The star that's heart shone~ The one who brought smiles to millions of peoples faces~

Many of you may know Cameron from the Disney channel movie series Descendant and he has starred in many more popular movies like Grownups and Grownups 2 and has starred in disney chanel shows Good luck Charlie, and Shake it up. And even played the starring role of Luke in Jessie and Bunk'd. But aside from being a great actor he was a caring person. He supported United Way of America's initiative to end homelessness. And he also helped bring clean water to undeveloped countries by working to raise $27,000 for the Thirst Project.Such talent and good heart will never be forgotten
